## 1. 概述

A complete, cross-platform solution to record, convert and stream audio and video.

官网：http://ffmpeg.org/

## 2. 编译环境

https://www.kaisawind.com/2019/11/19/2019-11-19-mysys/

### 2.2 必备软件

yasm、pkg-config、diffutils

```shell
pacman -S yasm pkg-config diffutils
```
