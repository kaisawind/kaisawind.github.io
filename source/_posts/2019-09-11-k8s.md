k8s批量删除pod

<!--more-->

```shell
kubectl delete pod $(kubectl get pod -n iot | grep broker | awk '{print $1}') -n iot
```

```makefile
@kubectl delete pod $$(kubectl get pod -n iot | grep $(IMAGE_NAME) | awk '{print $$1}') -n iot
```
