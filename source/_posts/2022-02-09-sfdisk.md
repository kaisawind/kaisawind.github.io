sfdisk: unsupported unit 'M'
<!--more-->

```bash
sfdisk --force $DRIVE -uM << EOF
10,500,0x0C
EOF
```
变更为
```bash
sfdisk --force $DRIVE << EOF
10M,500M,0x0C
EOF
```
